Models and Ifs

  • ng-model – two-way binding
  • ng-if – show element in DOM based on condition
  • ng-hide / ng-show – hide/show element visually based on condition
Template: 
<label><input type="checkbox" ng-model="ctrl.showInput"> Check me!</label><br>
<input ng-model="ctrl.myInput" ng-if="ctrl.showInput" placeholder="Write something...">
<span ng-show="ctrl.myInput.length > 0">You have written: <strong>{{ctrl.myInput}}</strong></span>
<input ng-model="ctrl.myInput" ng-show="ctrl.myInput" class="form-control">
